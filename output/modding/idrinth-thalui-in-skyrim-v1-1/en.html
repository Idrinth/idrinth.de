Idrinth Thalui in Skyrim v1.1

2026-04-04 0 views

One of the major mods I'm currently working on is Idrinth Thalui, the Altmer Vampire in Skyrim SE. Besides thousands of voice lines, own mechanics and a lot of things to discover, I decided the next version will be a bit more focused on the relaxing times outside combat.

The following things are already being worked on:

  • more singing, next to the current two songs
  • a singing competition with a specific orcish bard
  • some translation improvements

I have to be honest, I'm somewhat scared of the singing, since I'm not good at it. But then again we are going to finally have something he does without being good at it and it is just what happens if he relaxes. The following ideas are potentially being added:

  • singing when alone in a bathtub
  • recognising bath areas, so he stops putting on armor
  • more comments on children running around nearby

If you have more ideas, feel free to let me know on Discord!
