# Update on Nexusmods returning

I had been unable to login into Nexusmods for months, so there is quite a big backlog there. Great support from their team allowed me access again, so now I'm capable again of providing updates.

The first update I did was Idrinth Thalui, the Total War: Warhammer III version, to the version already on Steam Workshop. I expect maybe a download or two at most, but it just felt right to not force people to use the Steam version if they decided against it.

From a purely logical standpoint this investment of time makes little sense, there is just next to no audience for Total War: Warhammer III on nexusmods in comparison to the Steam Workshop. I know that, I still did the update and will continue to do updates to my mods no matter the game or platform for as long as I can.

Currently working on a few more things to push up soon, though I'm not sure if anyone there needs the Expanded Mod Configuration Tool or the Foreign Slot Fixes mods, they'll at least be available.
